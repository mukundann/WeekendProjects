🛠️ Substrate Engineering & Execution Workflow

  1. Surface Clearance (Jai, Giri, Mukundan): Drive a wide flat shovel across the targeted footprint to scrape back surface bark woodchips and expose underlying earth.
  2. Sub-Base Fracturing (Jai, Giri, Mukundan): Use a D-handle digging fork to penetrate the compacted native dirt base. Apply foot leverage to rock and crack open clay pan pathways.
  3. Weed Suppression (Team 1 + Kids): Place down dense, overlapping sheets of unprinted brown shipping cardboard over the fractured earth base and soak completely with water.
  4. Substrate Integration (Giri, Mukundan, Kids): Set up your modular open-bottom raised frames directly above the cardboard. Discharge your rich organic gardening soil bags and aeration potting mixes together inside the modular structure to create an airy, fertile growth zone.
