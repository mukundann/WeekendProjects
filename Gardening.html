🛠️ Garden Bed Frame Assembly & Site Prep Specifications

Detailed deployment steps for Team 1 to guarantee a structurally sound, level, and secure frame installation before adding the substrate.

  • Ground Leveling Calibration: Jai will check the cleared earth rectangle using a spirit level or smartphone leveling app. Giri and Mukundan will scrape high spots and fill dips with the flat shovel to ensure an absolutely level base, preventing future soil erosion or water pooling on one side.
  • Modular Panel Mechanical Connection: Lay out the metal side panels and corner joints on the concrete patio first. Connect the segments together using the provided wingnuts and bolts. Secure all fasteners hand-tight, then perform a final structural cross-turn using a screwdriver or pliers to lock the frame rigid.
  • Squaring and Final Alignment Check: Measure diagonally from corner to corner in an 'X' shape. If the two diagonal measurements are equal, the bed frame is perfectly square. Carefully transfer the assembled frame onto the leveled dirt footprint.
  • Base Anchoring: Drive the protective structural stabilization stakes down through the inside corners of the metal frame into the native earth using a rubber mallet or the back of a shovel to prevent shifting over time.

🔄 Substrate Engineering & Execution Workflow

  1. Surface Clearance (Jai, Giri, Mukundan): Drive a wide flat shovel across the targeted footprint to scrape back surface bark woodchips and expose underlying earth.
  2. Site Leveling & Frame Build (Team 1): Calibrate the ground grade level, assemble the modular metal panels on the patio, and seat the square frame securely on the bare dirt.
  3. Sub-Base Fracturing (Jai, Giri, Mukundan): Use a D-handle digging fork to penetrate the compacted native dirt base inside the frame. Apply foot leverage to rock and crack open clay pan pathways.
  4. Weed Suppression (Team 1 + Kids): Place down dense, overlapping sheets of unprinted brown shipping cardboard over the fractured earth base inside the frame and soak completely with water.
  5. Substrate Integration & Soil Layering (Giri, Mukundan, Kids): Discharge the specific purchased inventory inside the modular frame structure:
    • Empty the heavy bags of Sta-Green Garden Soil directly over the wet cardboard base layer to establish a dense, moisture-retaining organic foundation.
    • Layer the lightweight bags of Miracle-Gro Organic Potting Mix on top to maximize drainage and aeration properties.
    • Use a shovel or garden hand fork to churn and mix the two layers together until completely uniform, forming an airy, fertile growth zone.
